About David

Built for the Game.
Here for Your Game.

I'm David Schott, a pickleball instructor based in Colorado Springs, and I've spent the last five years helping players of every background fall in love with this sport. Whether you're stepping onto a court for the first time or grinding to break through your rating plateau, I meet you exactly where you are.

Colorado Springs has one of the most passionate pickleball communities in the state, and I'm proud to be part of it. My approach is hands-on and specific: we work on what actually costs you points, drill the patterns that create real improvement, and have a great time doing it.

If you're serious about getting better — or just serious about having more fun on the court — let's talk.

Got Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need any experience to work with you? +
Not at all. I coach players at every level — from people who have never stepped on a court to competitive players looking to climb the rankings. My Beginner Fundamentals program is designed specifically for those brand new to pickleball in Colorado Springs.
What equipment do I need to bring? +
If you have your own paddle and athletic shoes with non-marking soles, bring them. If you're just starting out, I can point you toward beginner-friendly gear. Pickleballs are provided for all sessions.
Where do lessons take place in Colorado Springs? +
I coach at several courts around Colorado Springs including Monument Valley Park, John Venezia Community Park, and Mid Shooks Run Park. The specific location depends on your session type and availability — we'll confirm when you book.
How long is each coaching session? +
Private sessions are typically 60 minutes. Group clinics run 90 minutes. Tournament Prep intensives can run up to 2 hours. I build in time for both drill work and applied play so every minute counts.
How quickly will I see improvement? +
Most players notice meaningful improvement in their very first session — there's always something specific we can fix right away. Consistent players who commit to a program typically see a half-point rating increase within 2–3 months.
What is your cancellation policy? +
I ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or reschedules. Life happens — just reach out and we'll find a time that works. Repeat no-shows may forfeit the session.
