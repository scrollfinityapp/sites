About Jeffrey

Coaching with Passion,
Purpose & Precision

Hi, I'm Jeffrey Santomango — a dedicated pickleball instructor based right here in Denver, Colorado. I've spent more than five years working with players of every level, from total beginners picking up a paddle for the first time to experienced competitors looking to sharpen their edge before a tournament.

Denver's pickleball community is one of the most enthusiastic I've ever seen, and I'm proud to be a part of its growth. Whether we're working on dinking consistency, third-shot drops, kitchen strategy, or footwork fundamentals, my sessions are built around you — your pace, your goals, and your game.

My coaching philosophy is simple: every player improves when they feel confident on the court. I break down complex mechanics into clear, actionable steps, so you leave every session with something concrete to build on. Let's get on the court and see what we can do together.
