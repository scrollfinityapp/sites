Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What paddle should I bring to my first lesson?
Don't worry about gear yet — I have loaner paddles you can use. Once we identify your playing style, I'll recommend the right paddle for you. Most beginners do fine with any mid-range composite paddle.
How long are lessons and how often should I train?
Private sessions are 60 minutes. For beginners, once a week is a great start. More serious players often train 2–3 times a week. We'll figure out a schedule that fits your life and your goals.
Do I need any experience to work with you?
None at all. I work with complete beginners through competitive tournament players. My beginner program is specifically designed for people who have never held a paddle.
Do you teach kids or just adults?
I primarily coach adults, but I do work with junior players age 12 and up. Reach out and we'll figure out if it's a good fit.
Where do sessions take place?
Sessions are held at local courts in Steamboat Springs. I'll send you the exact location when you book. Indoor facilities are available for rain days.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
Life happens. I just ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations so I can open the slot for another player. Same-day cancellations are charged at 50% of the session rate.
