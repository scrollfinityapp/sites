About Josh

Coached by someone who's played at the top.

PPR Certified Coach

I'm Josh Vinlove, a PPR Certified pickleball coach based in Aurora, Colorado. I've competed at the national level and bring that competitive experience to every lesson. Whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time or training for your next tournament, I focus on building solid technique, smart strategy, and the confidence to play your best game.

I teach at Arvada Pickleball and work with players of all ages and skill levels across the Aurora area. Teaching is what I do — on and off the court.
