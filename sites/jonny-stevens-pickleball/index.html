Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What paddle should I bring?

Don’t worry about gear — loaner paddles are available for all sessions. If you have your own paddle, feel free to bring it. I can also help you choose the right paddle for your style if you’re looking to buy.

How long are sessions and how often should I train?

Sessions are 60 minutes. For beginners, once a week is a great starting cadence. Competitive players typically train 2–3 times per week to see meaningful improvement.

Do I need any experience to sign up?

None at all. I work with players at every level — from people who’ve never held a paddle to seasoned competitors. Each session is tailored to where you are right now.

Do you teach kids?

My programs are primarily designed for adults. That said, juniors aged 12 and up are welcome — especially in private lessons or the Beginner Fundamentals program. Reach out to discuss your situation.

Where do sessions take place?

Sessions are held at local courts in Vail, including Golden Peak Pickleball Center and other nearby facilities. I’ll send you the exact location when you book. Indoor options are available during winter months.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please give at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule — no charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice are charged at 50% of the session rate. Same-day no-shows are charged in full.
