Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any experience to take lessons?

Not at all. Bob welcomes players at every level — from people who have never held a paddle to experienced competitors looking to sharpen their game. The Beginner Fundamentals program was built specifically for brand-new players, so you'll be in good hands from day one.

What equipment do I need to bring?

All you need is athletic footwear (court shoes are ideal), comfortable athletic clothing, and a water bottle. If you don't have a paddle yet, Bob can recommend beginner-friendly options and may have loaners available for your first session.

Where do lessons take place in Highlands Ranch?

Lessons are typically held at Southpark Pickleball Complex or Tanks Park Pickleball Courts in Highlands Ranch, CO. Indoor sessions may be arranged at Epic Pickleball Club. Bob will confirm the exact location when you book.

Are lessons available for kids?

Yes! Pickleball is an excellent sport for younger players. Bob adapts coaching style and drills to be age-appropriate and engaging. Private and small-group options are both available for junior players in the Highlands Ranch area.

What is your cancellation policy?

Life happens. Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a session. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. Reach out to Bob directly at rrspada@msn.com or (303) 944-9144 to reschedule.

How many sessions before I see improvement?

Most players notice measurable improvement within 2–3 sessions when they apply what they've learned. Bob's coaching is structured and progressive, so you'll build on each session. The four-session Beginner Fundamentals package is designed to give you a complete foundation by the end of the program.
