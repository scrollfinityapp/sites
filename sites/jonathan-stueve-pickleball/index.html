FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need any experience to book a session?
Not at all. The Beginner Fundamentals program is designed for players who've never touched a paddle. If you're brand new, that's the perfect starting point — we'll build your game from the ground up.
Where do coaching sessions take place in Colorado Springs?
Sessions are held at some of Colorado Springs' best public courts, including Monument Valley Park and John Venezia Community Park. Court location is confirmed when you book based on availability and your preference.
What equipment do I need to bring?
Just bring yourself and athletic shoes. Paddles and balls are available to borrow for your first session if you don't have your own gear yet. We can also talk about equipment recommendations for your skill level.
How often should I take lessons to improve?
For most players, one session per week paired with two to three hours of open play produces noticeable results within a month. More serious players preparing for tournaments often do two or three sessions per week.
Can I book a session as a gift for someone in Colorado Springs?
Absolutely. Gift sessions are a popular option. Just reach out via email and mention it's a gift — I'll make sure everything is set up smoothly for the recipient.
What's your cancellation policy?
Life happens. Just give me at least 24 hours notice and we'll reschedule at no charge. Late cancellations or no-shows on the day of the session are subject to a cancellation fee.
